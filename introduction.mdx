# Welcome to Cluely

Cluely is an invisible desktop assistant that provides real-time insights, answers, and support during meetings, interviews, presentations, and professional conversations. Built with Electron and React, it runs as a translucent, always-on-top window that seamlessly integrates with your workflow.

## What makes Cluely special?

Cluely is designed to be your silent partner in high-stakes situations. Whether you're in a technical interview, client presentation, or learning session, Cluely analyzes screenshots and provides AI-powered insights without being noticed.

## Key features

### Invisible AI assistant

Cluely runs as a translucent, always-on-top window that's barely noticeable during use. Toggle visibility instantly with global hotkeys (`Cmd/Ctrl + B`) or hide completely when not needed.

### Smart screenshot analysis

Capture any content on your screen with `Cmd/Ctrl + H`. Cluely's AI analyzes:
- Code problems and error messages
- Technical diagrams and architecture
- Presentation slides and documents
- Math equations and scientific content
- Interview questions and prompts

Screenshots are automatically processed and deleted after analysis for privacy.

### Contextual chat interface

Maintain ongoing conversations with the AI about captured content. Ask follow-up questions, request clarifications, or dive deeper into topics without switching contexts.

### Audio intelligence

Process audio files and recordings for:
- Real-time transcription
- Meeting notes and summaries
- Content analysis and insights

### Privacy-focused architecture

Choose your privacy level:
- **Maximum Privacy**: Use Ollama for 100% local AI processing with no cloud dependencies
- **Hybrid**: Mix local and cloud providers based on task sensitivity
- **Performance**: Use cloud providers like Gemini for maximum speed and accuracy

All screenshots are automatically deleted after processing. No data tracking or persistent storage.
